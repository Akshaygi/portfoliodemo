Traditional Post-Production Meets AI

Post-production doesn’t have to follow the same traditional formula. While we carrying on the consistency and quality that is required in post-production, we’ve broken down the way in which consistency and quality is achieved with a Specialist Assisted Workflow (SAW). Post-production is broken into small steps, some of which are performed by AI or automation, while post-production experts handle more intense or subjective tasks. This combination of traditional and AI has led to speed and consistency.
